Project Overview

Rainbow County is an innovative residential plotted development that brings a vibrant and colorful living experience to the serene Srisailam Highway corridor. This unique project is designed to create a harmonious blend of nature's beauty and modern living, with each zone featuring themed gardens and landscapes inspired by the colors of the rainbow.

Spread across 35 acres of meticulously planned landscape, Rainbow County offers a living experience unlike any other. Each residential zone is color-themed with corresponding flowering plants, creating a visually stunning environment that changes with the seasons while providing a serene and uplifting atmosphere for residents.

Our vision for Rainbow County is to create not just plots, but a complete lifestyle community where families can thrive amidst nature's vibrant palette. With premium amenities, eco-friendly practices, and thoughtful planning, this project represents the future of residential living where color, nature, and community come together in perfect harmony.

The Color-Themed Zones

Experience living in a rainbow with seven distinct color zones

Red Rose Zone

Featuring vibrant red flowering plants including roses, hibiscus, and bougainvillea. Perfect for those who love passion and energy in their surroundings.

Orange Blossom Zone

Beautiful orange marigolds, lilies, and trumpet vines creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere that energizes your day.

Yellow Sunshine Zone

Sunflowers, yellow roses, and golden trumpet trees bringing sunshine to your day with their cheerful and bright appearance.

Green Harmony Zone

Lush green lawns, ferns, and evergreen plants creating a peaceful and rejuvenating environment for relaxation and meditation.

Blue Sky Zone

Blue hydrangeas, morning glories, and plumbago creating a cool, serene ambiance that calms the mind and soothes the soul.

Indigo Twilight Zone

Indigo flowers, lavender, and jacaranda trees for a mystical evening atmosphere that inspires creativity and contemplation.

Violet Dream Zone

Violet orchids, lilacs, and wisteria creating a royal and luxurious ambiance that sparks imagination and dreams.
